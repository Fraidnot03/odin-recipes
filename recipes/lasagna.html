Lasagna

Description:

This recipe is about the classic Italian dish "Lasagna". This dish in not only delicious, it is easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Cheese
  • Layers of Pasta
  • Garlic
  • Butter

Steps:

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Prepare uncooked pasta layers in an oven pan
  3. Layer cooked beef and pasta and cheese
  4. Add more cheese at the top
  5. Bake in the oven
  6. Serve

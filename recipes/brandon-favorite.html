Brandon's Favorite

Description:

This dish is Brandon's favorite dish of all time. Since a kid his mother made this and has alway smacked. It is a very simple dish that requires not much energy to create. It is essentially beef-stroganoff but simplified.

Ingredients:

  • White Rice
  • Campbell's Muschroom Soup
  • Your choice of beef
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Milk

Steps:

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Cook the rice
  3. season the beef while both are cooking
  4. Once the beef is cooked then add the mushroom soup to the beef
  5. Add a little bit of milk
  6. Once both are done cooking then place rice in a bowl and pour the beef with the soup on top
